You can‘t see it here, but I loop through ActionView::Helpers::FormBuilder::field_helpers and redefine the field_helpers, plus datetime_select and date_select, minus the check_box and radio_button:

  • text_field
  • file_field
  • password_field
  • hidden_field
  • text_area
  • datetime_select
  • date_select

use them like you normally would, but I add two booleans to the options hash:

disable_behavior:reverts to normal behavior, inside of labeled_form_for
required:marks the field as required with an asterisk and css class name
for example
f.text_field :first_name
will produce
<dl> 
  <dt>
    <label for="user_first_name">First Name</label>
  </dt>
  <dd>
    <input id="user_first_name" type="text" size="30" name="user[first_name]" accesskey="f"/>
  </dd>

<dl>
